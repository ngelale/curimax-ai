**Application Status Legend**:
- 🟡 **Under Review**: Committee is evaluating your application
- 🟢 **Interview Scheduled**: You've been selected for an interview
- 🟠 **Action Required**: You need to submit additional materials
- ✅ **Accepted**: Congratulations! You've been admitted
- ❌ **Denied**: Unfortunately, you were not admitted this time
- ⏸️ **Waitlisted**: You're on the waitlist, may be admitted later
- 🚫 **Withdrawn**: You withdrew your application
- ⏱️ **Incomplete**: Application started but not submitted
