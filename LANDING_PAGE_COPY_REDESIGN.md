## SECTION 6: HOW IT WORKS (3-5 Steps)

### Step 1: Tell Us About Your Program (2 minutes)
```
You fill out a quick intake form:
- Program name, level (certificate/bachelor/master)
- Target job roles or industries
- Target location/region
- Current enrollment (if existing) or target size

No technical setup, no data CSV uploads. Just structured questions that take 
as long as a coffee break.
```

### Step 2: Our AI Analyzes the Market (10-15 minutes)
```
Behind the scenes, our platform:
- Searches 2M+ recent job postings for your target roles
- Checks BLS and O*NET labor demand forecasts
- Identifies 20-50 competitor programs in your market
- Calculates financial viability (revenue, costs, break-even)
- Generates market opportunity scenarios

You get a status email when it's ready. Grab a coffee — it's happening.
```

### Step 3: Download Your Report (3 minutes)
```
You get a professional PDF/PowerPoint with:
- Executive summary (1 page, ready for board)
- Market opportunity analysis (job demand, growth outlook)
- Competitive landscape (who else offers this, how they price it)
- Financial model (enrollment scenarios, revenue/cost projections)
- Top 10 strategic recommendations

All formatted and ready to present. No editing needed.
```

### Optional Step 4: Share & Refine
```
For institutional teams:
- Share reports with stakeholders (board, faculty committee, finance team)
- Collaborate on recommendations
- Run "what-if" scenarios ("what if we charge $2K/month instead of $1.5K?")
```

### Optional Step 5: Build & Launch
```
Use report to:
- Finalize program design
- Make pricing/enrollment decisions
- Brief your board
- Execute launch confidently

(Curriculum.ai helps with templates; students/institutions handle the actual 
program build)
```


- Blue = trust, intelligence, technology (education/AI context)
